Welcome to BDMVS

The Biological Development Management & Visualization System (BDMVS) is a comprehensive tool designed to manage and visualize biological data. It enables users to search, analyze, and retrieve protein-related information from a vast database. The system offers easy access to data, allowing users to explore key biological metrics and visualize complex protein structures. With a user-friendly interface, BDMVS aims to streamline biological research by providing efficient and interactive tools for data analysis and visualization.
